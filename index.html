Prime number checker

Here you can test wether a number is prime number or not.

Just type your number in, click on "Set as number" and decide for one check. I recommend the normal check.

Easy mode

The Easy mode just tells you wether your number is a prime number or not.

Normal mode

The Normal mode tells you what numbers from 2-9 your number is divsible by, whether it is a prime number or not and it calculates the results and display them in the console.

Advanced mode

The Advanced mode tells you

  • wether your number is a prime number or not,
  • What numbers from 2-9 your number is divisible by,
  • the results of the calculation (in the console),
  • if it is not a prime number, it tells you the next two prime numbers.

Check numbers in a range



This check can check all numbers between the starting and ending number for prime numbers. Attention: Do not make the distance over circa 20 000 to prevent a crash on old systems and mobile devices.
